About Us

Designing a space does not only require a visual approach, but it is about the process of thinking itself, about skills, aspects that helped us define as our professional goal the improvement of the quality and reality of our clients' lives through the projects we work on. All interior design projects are based on understanding the client, the one whose state of mind is influenced by the environment in which he lives, works or engages in relaxation activities. His ideas, requirements and wishes are found in the project and are stylized and transformed into functional, aesthetic ideas and are properly approached from a technical point of view. When we "get to know" a new workspace, we take the time to analyze and feel all its transformation possibilities, we look carefully for the signs that indicate how much potential there is for change for the better, and we try to find as many strong points as possible to start the concept. We believe that any defect or supposed minus can take a positive form or can be enhanced by another element not identified until that moment. Situations also happen during a project that are challenging for us as a designer and this makes our work more dynamic, more exciting, helps us to surpass ourself and learn continuously. We believe that there is at least one solution for any potential problem.

Interior design project process

1. Meeting the client and visiting the space During this meeting, the requirements and the work strategy are broadly established.

2. Stage 1 - Concept Project Documentation, General furnishing ideas, Establishing the design style, establishing the chromatic range, creating a mood board - indications of materials and suggestive pictures to obtain all the visual information.

3. Stage 2 - RLV and Rendering RLV - Realization of specific measurements on the spot.

4. Stage 3 - Sketches and plans Dimensioned 2d and 3d furniture sketches - precise sketches for the execution of custom-made furniture and indications of materials and finishes depending on the allocated budget.

5. Coordination and execution We assist the work team on site at the predetermined stages and make sure that the technical plans are respected, and the project is carried out 1:1 with the sketches and renderings.
